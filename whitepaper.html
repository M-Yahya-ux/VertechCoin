Executive Summary

VertechCoin represents a revolution in how business innovation is financed. Our token connects global capital with entrepreneurial talent through a hybrid financing model that combines traditional cash with utility tokens.

In an ecosystem where investors seek diversified opportunities and entrepreneurs need accessible capital, VertechCoin acts as the bridge that makes this connection possible in a transparent, efficient way aligned with the incentives of the entire community.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To create the world's most transparent and efficient financial ecosystem, where any entrepreneur with a valuable idea can access the necessary capital and any investor can participate in the future of innovation.

Our Mission

To democratize access to venture capital through blockchain technology, eliminating geographical and bureaucratic barriers, while creating a financing model that aligns the incentives of investors and entrepreneurs in the long term.

03

Problem We Solve

For Entrepreneurs
  • Limited access to venture capital
  • Long and bureaucratic processes
  • Lack of transparency in evaluation
  • Geographic concentration of capital
For Investors
  • High entry barriers (minimum tickets)
  • Lack of accessible diversification
  • Asymmetric information
  • Expensive due diligence processes
04

The VertechCoin Solution

VertechCoin introduces a revolutionary hybrid financing model that combines the best of traditional and decentralized finance:

Hybrid Financing

Projects can set goals in both cash (€) and VertechCoin, allowing multiple forms of participation and creating a more flexible ecosystem.

Total Transparency

All transactions, overflow rules and allocations are visible on-chain, eliminating the traditional opacity of venture capital.

Aligned Community

VertechCoin holders become active participants in the ecosystem, with voting rights on key decisions and preferential access to new projects.

Global Access

Without geographical restrictions, anyone in the world can participate as an investor or entrepreneur, democratizing access to capital.

06

Token Utility

The VertechCoin token is at the heart of the ecosystem, powering investments, governance, and access.

Investment Medium

VertechCoin serves as the native currency to invest in projects listed on the platform, creating a closed and sustainable economic cycle.

Tier Access

Holders get early access, better ticket limits and exclusive perks based on the amount of VertechCoin they own.

Governance

Voting rights on key decisions: which verticals to prioritize, matching pool usage and featured projects on the platform.

Staking

Optional staking functionality that grants allocation priority in projects with high demand and reduced liquidity.

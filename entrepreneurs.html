Excess Management

Practical example: Your project asks for 100,000 VertechCoin and $50,000. If VertechCoin is worth $0.50, the token part equals $50,000.
If at closing VertechCoin rises to $0.75, those 100,000 VertechCoin would equal $75,000. Excess = $25,000

Pro-rata

The amount of VertechCoin accepted is reduced to meet exactly the target.

Result: You receive exactly what was planned, return the excess.

Cap + Redistribution

A +20% cap is respected. The excess is redistributed according to agreed policy.

Result: More runway for marketing and development.

Cap + Perks

What exceeds the cap is converted into extra perks/benefits for investors.

Result: Greater value for the investor community.
